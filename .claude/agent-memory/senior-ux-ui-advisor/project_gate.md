Premium commercial real estate landing page for the last 5 floors of GATE Jerusalem Tower B2 (40 stories).

**Why:** BSR Group / JTLV Fund need to sell the remaining inventory. Each floor is 31-40M NIS. The page must operate as a high-trust lead generation machine, not a brochure.

**How to apply:** Every design decision should be evaluated against two criteria: (1) does it reduce perceived risk for a 30-40M NIS purchase decision, and (2) does it create urgency without feeling cheap.

### Business Goal
Generate qualified leads (serious buyers/investors) for 5 remaining floors. Secondary: build credibility for the developer ecosystem (BSR, JTLV, Yehuda Rahamim).

### Target Audiences (priority order)
1. Israeli companies expanding/relocating to Jerusalem — emotional + practical
2. Tech companies/startups — ROI via government grants (up to 7.2M NIS)
3. Real estate investors — floor as asset, appreciation, yield
4. International firms — Israel HQ, proximity to government
5. Government contractors — Knesset/courts adjacency

### Competitive Positioning
- Not just a building — part of a 300-dunam urban quarter (1.2M sqm total)
- Israel's largest integrated transport hub (train 32min TLV, 3 light rail lines, 280 bus routes)
- LEED green building, D-BLK Architects (international, 20+ countries)
- Only 5 floors remain — genuine scarcity
- Floor 24 unique premium: all 13 elevators reach it
