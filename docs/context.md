# Gate Jerusalem - Project Context & Data

## Project Identity

**Project Name:** GATE Jerusalem (שער העיר / Sha'ar Ha'ir)
**Building:** B2 Office Tower
**Tagline:** "The New Gate of Jerusalem" — Israel's next business capital

## Developers & Partners

- **B.S.R. Group** (בסר) — Lead developer. Chairman: Nachshon Kiyutsky. Previous projects: BSR towers 1-4 in Bnei Brak, BSR City in Petach Tikva, Alon towers in Tel Aviv.
- **JTLV Fund** — Real estate investment fund, 60% ownership stake.
- **Yehuda Rahamim Company** — Development partner.
- **Architect:** BLK Architects (led by Moti Kesif)
- **Landscape:** TOPOTEK1 (international firm)
- **Lending Bank:** Bank Hapoalim (בנק הפועלים)
- **Marketing/Branding:** Brave agency

## Location

- Jerusalem's entrance district (רובע הכניסה לירושלים)
- Beneath the iconic String Bridge (גשר המיתרים)
- Adjacent to National Buildings complex
- Walking distance to Machane Yehuda Market and Old City
- Opposite the central bus station

## The Larger Development (Jerusalem Gateway Quarter)

- **Total area:** ~300 dunams
- **Total built rights:** 1.2 million sqm
- **Investment:** 1.4 billion NIS in infrastructure
- **Employment capacity:** 60,000 jobs
- **Daily pedestrian traffic:** 350,000
- **Buildings:** 20 towers up to 40 stories
- **Space allocation:**
  - ~650,000 sqm employment
  - ~200,000 sqm hospitality
  - ~100,000 sqm residential
  - ~100,000 sqm public/cultural use
  - ~72,000 sqm commerce
  - ~70,000 sqm rental housing
  - ~25,000 sqm subsidized housing

## GATE Office Tower (B2) — Specifications

- **Height:** 40 stories
- **Total office space:** ~60,000 sqm
- **Elevators:** 13 high-speed elevators
- **Parking:** Underground parking, 8 parking spots per floor
- **Parking price:** 250,000 NIS + VAT per spot
- **Building standard:** LEED green building certification
- **Two zones:** Low Zone (lower floors) and High Zone (upper floors)
- **Floor 24 is unique:** All elevators reach this floor

## Additional Complex Components

- ~350 residential apartments
- ~35,000 sqm hotel
- ~8,000 sqm commercial/retail
- ~6,100 sqm culture & community
- Second 40-story tower (planned)
- European-style commercial promenade/boulevard

## Amenities

- Impressive lobbies
- High-speed elevators
- Gym/fitness center
- Synagogue
- Flexible, modular office layouts (small offices to full floors)
- LEED green building standards

## Transportation — Israel's Largest Integrated Transport Hub

- **Rail:** Yitzhak Navon train station — 32 minutes to Tel Aviv
- **Light Rail:** 3 lines (Red active; Green and Blue planned)
- **Bus:** 280 urban and intercity routes via adjacent central bus station
- **Cycling:** 2 km of new bicycle paths
- **Parking:** Sha'ar parking lot (public)

## Institutional & Cultural Anchors Nearby

- Supreme Court & courts complex
- Knesset (Parliament)
- Government offices
- Bezalel Academy of Arts & Design (new campus)
- Museums
- National Library (planned)

- **Net area per floor (35, 37):** ~1,249 sqm

- **Parking:** 8 spots per floor at 250,000 NIS + VAT each = 2,000,000 NIS + VAT per floor
- **Floor 24 uniqueness:** All building elevators reach this floor (premium access)

Companies establishing offices in Jerusalem can receive:
- Grants up to **600,000 NIS**
- Growth companies: up to **7.2 million NIS**
- Biotech firms: up to **4 million NIS**
- **Reduced municipal property tax (arnona) rates**
