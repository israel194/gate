# Gate Jerusalem — Landing Page Plan

## Objective

A Hebrew (RTL) landing page to sell 5 available office floors (21, 22, 24, 35, 37) in Building B2 of the GATE Jerusalem complex. The page targets investors, business owners, and companies looking for premium office space in Jerusalem's new business district.

---

## Target Audience

1. **Israeli companies** expanding to Jerusalem or relocating HQ
2. **Tech companies & startups** attracted by government grants (up to 7.2M NIS)
3. **International firms** seeking Israel office presence
4. **Real estate investors** — office floors as investment assets
5. **Government contractors** — proximity to Knesset, courts, ministries

---

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style
- **Direction:** RTL (Hebrew)
- **Color palette:** Deep navy (#1a2744), gold accent (#c9a84c), white, light gray
- **Typography:** Modern Hebrew font (e.g., Heebo or Assistant from Google Fonts)
- **Imagery:** Architectural renderings, aerial shots of Jerusalem, interior office mockups
- **Tone:** Premium, professional, confident — not flashy

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Hero video scales or becomes static image on mobile
- Floor cards stack vertically on mobile
- Sticky CTA button on mobile (fixed bottom)
- Contact form easily accessible

### Performance
- Lazy-load images and video
- Optimize renders as WebP
- Minify CSS/JS
- Target < 3s load time

---

## Tech Stack (Recommended)

- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) with static export or SSG
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with RTL plugin
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (counter animations, scroll reveals)
- **Form handling:** Web3Forms or server action → email notification
- **Maps:** Google Maps Embed API
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel (for retargeting)
- **Chat:** WhatsApp floating button

## Conversion Optimization

1. **Urgency:** "5 קומות אחרונות" prominently displayed
2. **Social proof:** Developer track record, Bank Hapoalim backing
3. **Anchoring:** Show per-sqm price before total (20,500 NIS feels more accessible than 31.7M)
4. **Risk reduction:** 30/70 payment terms, bank guarantee
5. **Value-add:** Government grants section — floor cost partially offset
6. **Multiple CTAs:** Hero, after pricing, after grants, floating button
7. **Exit intent popup:** "לפני שעוזבים — השאירו פרטים לקבלת מצגת הפרויקט" (Before you leave — get the project presentation)
